Chef's Story

Chef Mira keeps the fire personal.

Chef Mira Rao grew up around weekend markets, railway snacks, and family dinners that lasted late into the night. Her menu keeps those memories close while adding a clean, modern dining-room finish.

Every sauce is built from whole spices, every bread is fired to order, and every plate is designed to bring the table into the conversation.

Our Story

Classic recipes, city-night polish.

Saffron Table began with family recipes from Delhi and Mumbai, then grew into a dining room that balances comfort with occasion. The kitchen keeps the big flavors intact: toasted spices, long-simmered sauces, charred breads, and dishes that arrive ready to pass around.

Come for a quick lunch thali, stay for dinner with friends, or settle at the bar for a cardamom old fashioned and crisp pakoras.
